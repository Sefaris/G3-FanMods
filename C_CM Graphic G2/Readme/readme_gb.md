Requiments: Content Mod Base

This modification adds graphical changes to ContentMod in the style of Gothic 2.

Changes:
- New appearance of paladin and Royal Guard armor;
- Some NPCs will have crawler plate armor, Knight Armor and militia armor;
- Retexture of knight's shield and paladin's shield;
- Hanged bodies of paladins in orcish cities have been replaced with the bodies of militiamen;
- New appearance of the undead in Gotha;
- Old acquaintances from Gothic 2 are dressed in their recognizable armor (Lester, Lares, Lee, Gorn, Diego, Angar, Thorus, Xardas);
- Some bandits are dressed in bandit clothes from Gothic 2.
