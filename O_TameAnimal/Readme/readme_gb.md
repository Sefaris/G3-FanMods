Requirements: Main

Adds the ability to tame animals after learning new perks. A tamed animal is neutral to other NPCs. It can be fed to restore its health.

When learning the "Pack Leader" skill, the number of animals that can be tamed becomes unlimited.

Boars and swamplurkers no longer rush at the player without warning.

The varant is filled with gazelles from the LTM mod - author Yuko.

Several new enemies appeared in one of the caves - author Adamtech.
