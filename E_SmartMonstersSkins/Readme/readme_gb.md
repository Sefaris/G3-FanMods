Retexture of animals and monsters. Some textures are improved to 4k resolution, others have a completely updated texture. Changed textures of Lurkers, Snappers, lions, lizards, rhinos, sabertooths, skeletons, Rippers, goblins, turtles and meatbugs.

Note: When installing this mod from ContentModBase, keep in mind that the sabertooths in Nordmar will become orange again.
