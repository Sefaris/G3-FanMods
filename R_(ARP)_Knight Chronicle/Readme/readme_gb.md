Requirements: Main

A mod that tells the story of an unknown knight and his travels through Myrtana.

Features of the mod:
- A large quest line with different endings
- New NPCs in Myrtana and Varant, as well as unique enemies.
- New model of Shadowbeast from Arcania (replacement of the original)
- 4 new types of monsters with unique trophies
- Retexture of the Fire Golem 
- New appearance of Cor-Angar 
- 19 new armors
-4 helmets
- 20 new types of weapons (one-handed, two-handed swords, halberds, staves, crossbows, bows, etc.)
- New crafting recipes, items, arrows, etc.
- The mod is fully voiced in Russian
