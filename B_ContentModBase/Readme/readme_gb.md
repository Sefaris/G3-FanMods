Base part of Content Mod. This modification is necessary for the other mods down the line to work. The changes that will be noticeable in the game with this mod are completely based on the materials of the original Gothic 3 and the Forsaken Gods add-on.

List of changes:
- More diversity in the armors worn by Nordmarians;
- More diversity in the armors worn by Hashishin;
- More diversity in the armors worn by mercenaries;
- Female rebels and mercenaries;
- Robes for the novices of the Monastery;
- Headdresses and helmets for NPCs around the world;
- A darker background of parchment for letters and books;
- New heads of Lares, Inog, Anog, Javier, Vatras;
- Unique bodies for the leaders of the Nordmar clans and druids;
- Changed appearance of the orcish armor;
- Unique appearance of Kahn;
- New zombie appearance;
- Loot drops from mummies and cursed priests;
- Higher quality texture of the main character, head in 4K;
- New names for standard NPCs.
