Requirement: CM Content

This modification supplements the main Content Mod with two mini-quests without new dialogues, and also adds various lore books and notes from the authors of Content Mod to the world.

List:
- Quest for the Sword of the Elements
- Quest for Knight's armor
- Amulet of transformation into a demon
- Stone of transformation into a dragon
- Note of the rebel scout
- Guardsman's armor on Kobrin's body
- Heavy armor of the militia on Russell's body
- Map and diary of Ortega
- Books about Xardas
- Diary of Sigmor
- Book about dragons
- Book about Varant
- Book about Nordmar
- Book about gods
- Book about druids
- Book about General Lee
- Books about the war with the orcs
- Horned helmet in the mine of the Hammer Clan
