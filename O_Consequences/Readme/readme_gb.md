Requirement: Main

The modification adds events to the game, caused by the actions of the hero, which may seem "hostile" to one or another side of the conflict. New dialogues, atmospheric details, new characters and quests have been added to the game. This version of the mod is with the effects of LTM. Numerous bugs and shortcomings have been fixed. You can choose the difficulty level of the mod in the initial dialogue with Hamlar or Tristan.
