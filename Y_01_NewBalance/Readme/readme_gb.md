This mod is not compatible with the following mods: CombatFix

Comprehensive rework of game balance and combat system:
- Changed NPC and monster damage formulas
- Some spells with a long cast have a slight immunity to interruption
- Stronger units have immunity to interruption from weaker units
- Reworked stamina consumption for the hero, NPCs and mobs
- Changed armor action formulas, some types of attacks and weapons will ignore armor to a greater or lesser extent
- NPCs can use different types of arrows and bolts, depending on their level and class
- Block works the same as in the classic combat system, parrying is implemented for a well-timed block
- Changed damage formulas for different types of attacks: for example, slashing attacks do more damage, fast ones - less
- Various bonuses to one or another type of damage for NPCs and mobs. For example, skeletons take more damage from crushing weapons, golems take less damage from a blade, etc.
- Changed damage formulas for the hero depending on the weapon: two-handed swords receive 0.55 additional damage from strength, axes - 0.6. Weapons that require dexterity receive a bonus of 0.2 from strength and 0.4 from dexterity. Dual blades receive a bonus of 0.3 from strength and 0.35 from dexterity. Staves receive a bonus of 0.2 from strength and 0.4 from intelligence
- Reworked system of status effects: ice weapons are more likely to freeze enemies, the hero receives more immunity to status effects if fire and frost protection skills are learned
- The spell banish evil works against dragons
And others.
